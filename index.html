Privacy Policy for Subdivide

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Subdivide ("the App") is a metronome and rhythm-practice tool developed as an independent project. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

Subdivide does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking software development kits (SDKs), and it does not require an account, login, or internet connection to function.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your settings — such as tempo, time signature, subdivision depth, sound choices, and saved presets — are stored locally on your device. This information stays on your device and is not accessible to the developer.

In-App Purchases

Subdivide offers an optional one-time "Subdivide Pro" purchase, processed entirely through Apple's App Store and StoreKit. Payment information is handled by Apple in accordance with Apple's privacy policy and is never seen by the developer.

Third Parties

Subdivide does not share information with third parties because it does not collect any information to share.

Children's Privacy

Subdivide does not collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes (for example, if a future version of the App adds optional analytics or cloud sync), this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to: subdivide.app@gmail.com
