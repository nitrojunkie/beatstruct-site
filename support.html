Beatstruct Support

Metronome & Polyrhythm trainer for iPhone

Need help, found a bug, or have a feature request? We're happy to hear from you.

Contact

Email subdivide.app@gmail.com and we'll get back to you, usually within a few days. Please include your iPhone model and iOS version if you're reporting a problem.

Common questions

  • Restoring a purchase — open Settings → Restore Purchases to re-activate Beatstruct Pro on a new device or after reinstalling.
  • No sound — check that your device isn't on silent / Focus mode, and that the volume is up. Beatstruct plays through the normal media channel.
  • Background audio — the metronome keeps playing when the screen locks or another app is in front.

Privacy

Beatstruct collects no personal data. See the Privacy Policy for details.
